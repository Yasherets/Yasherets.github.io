SONS OF VELES

Some heroes live long enough to become the villains. None symbolize this concept better or more brutally than the outlaw faction known as Sons of Veles.

The tale, now rightly called myth, of the Earth Special Forces ace codenamed “Leshii”, the only man alive to take down a Witherer, is a tale of glory, turmoil - and regret. Fresh from the greatest human military victory, Leshii and his unit returned...changed. They renounced the Earth Government’s rule on behalf of humanity, vaporised the forces welcoming them home, and disappeared into space, to resurface as the neo-pagan warrior cult Sons of Veles, a thorn in Earth’s expansion plans, and their most hated enemy. Operating on a secret code of honour, Sons of Veles are few, but they possess experimental melee-focused technology unavailable to any other faction - as well as some of the most ruthless, skilled pilots known to man. So far, they have exhibited no particular agenda aside from brutalising Earth's forces - but that may be about to change.
