THE CARCOSANS

Like yellow sulphurous smoke out of an infernal chasm, the alien entities known as the Carcosans poured forth out of an abandoned Witherer rift at the edge of the galaxy. Striking, frightening, and powerful, with motivations entirely outside of human comprehension, their custom mechs of eldritch design stalk the fraying edges of known space like restless wraiths, waiting for...something. Those few who have dared, by intent or unhappy accident, to venture close to Carcosan domain, report haunting, nonsensical communication, visceral feelings of dread, and always, dancing just beyond the edges of their vision - and their sanity - a ragged, crowned figure in yellow, beckoning them ever onward.
