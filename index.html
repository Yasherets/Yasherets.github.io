ABOUT

After a century of rebuilding and isolation, venture back into the unfamiliar solar system that mankind used to call home.

GAME NAME is a turn-based strategy roguelike where you unlock the secrets of the new solar system with the assistance of customizable and upgradable mechs and technology. There is no structure or order in space, every playthrough will tell a different story.

RECLAIM THE SYSTEM

In the 23rd century, an ancient and advanced race named the Witherers attacked the solar system and laid waste to Earth and its colonies. Leaving as suddenly as they arrived, pockets of the human race across space were left stranded and divided. After 100 dark and desperate years, the divided factions of the solar system now return to space in search of truth, power, and vengeance. But the solar system is not what it once was. In the wake of the Witherers, strange anomalies and dangerous lifeforms now inhabit the system. Even the other human survivors are not who they once were.
